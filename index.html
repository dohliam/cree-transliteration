Canadian Aboriginal Syllabics (Cree) transliteration

This converter transliterates to and from Canadian Aboriginal Syllabics (Cree) and Standard Roman Orthography (SRO). To use, simply enter some text in Canadian Aboriginal Syllabics or SRO into the Input box, optionally select the input orthography from the dropdown menu, and press the Convert button. The transliterated text will appear in the Output box below.
